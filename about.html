Our HISTORY

Our love for dogs has become our job. We put ourselves in the place of all parents (of dogs), in pain and in the concern to leave their pets alone on trips for work and fun and we decided to create a solution for that. DOGinn appeared for the first moment to meet this demand and today it has become a reference in a hotel for dogs.

Now you don’t have to worry about going out or traveling and not be able to take your dog with you leaving them behind. Bringing him to DOGinn you will be sure that he will have as much fun as you do.
